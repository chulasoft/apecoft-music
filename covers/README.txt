Put your cover images here (1000x1000, 1:1 recommended).

- Album covers  -> referenced by the "cover" column in data/albums.csv
- Song covers   -> referenced by the "cover" column in data/songs.csv (optional)
  If a song's cover is blank, it uses its album cover automatically.
- Artist portraits (900x1200+) -> referenced by the "image" column in data/artists.csv
